Unfortunately, this means that a lot of substandard films pop up in our recommended lists.

We analyse the films and upload our reviews as fast as possible so our readers can make the right picks for their viewing pleasure. We also upload weekly reviews of classic films which some of our readers might not be aware of. These reviews are an opportunity for us to shed light on forgotten cinematic gems which our readers can enjoy. Our constant aim is to foster the community of film fans through our website and promote the appreciation of great films.
